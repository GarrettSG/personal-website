# About Meritage Homes

## What is Meritage Homes?

Meritage Homes Corporation is the fifth-largest and most innovative public homebuilder in the United States, 
known for pioneering energy-efficient homebuilding practices and creating high-quality, affordable, and 
sustainable communities. Founded in 1985 and headquartered in Scottsdale, Arizona, Meritage has built and 
sold over 175,000 homes across the country.

## Where Are We?

Operations span across Arizona, California, Colorado, Utah, Texas, Florida, Georgia, North Carolina, South
Carolina, and Tennessee.
